Why do we end up following the stream of thought?<br/>

Why do my own actions stay with me more than the events of the game?<br/>
</QuestionBlock>
Somewhere along the way, I came down with what people call "main-character syndrome."<br/>
It wasn't that I was the protagonist — I'd sunk completely into the game's protagonist.
<br/>
The choices I made after settling on a concept chained together like a butterfly effect, and in the end<br/>
it all closed with death.<br/>
It was a shock.
<br/>
The strangest part:<br/>
I was the one choosing,<br/>
yet the basis for every choice had already been tuned to the protagonist's character.
<br/>
The more I played, the more these questions stayed with me.
